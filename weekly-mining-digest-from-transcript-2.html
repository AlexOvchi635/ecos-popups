Metaplanet: Japan's Bitcoin Bet
Japanese investment company Metaplanet saw its stock price soar nearly 90x after adopting a Bitcoin strategy. The company plans to buy up to 210,000 BTC by 2027, aiming to become the world's second-largest corporate holder after MicroStrategy.

Metaplanet's pivot to Bitcoin is driven by Japan's high government debt, negative interest rates, and a weak yen. Experts note that these economic risks are pushing institutions to view Bitcoin as a hedge against sovereign default.

However, this strategy is not without risk: Metaplanet's market cap is still much smaller than MicroStrategy's, and heavy reliance on debt could be dangerous if the market turns. The company also owns a hotel in Tokyo and holds the exclusive license for Bitcoin Magazine in Japan.
