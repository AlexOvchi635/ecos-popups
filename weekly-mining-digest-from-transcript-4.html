Bitcoin Holds Steady — ETF Flows Turn Positive

Bitcoin is trading around $66,000, with Ethereum near $1,940. Crypto prices held in a narrow range over the weekend — a brief dip to $63k was followed by a quick recovery to prior levels. For the week ending Feb 27, US spot Bitcoin ETFs recorded a net inflow of $787M, ending five consecutive weeks of outflows. Ethereum ETFs attracted $80M in the same period.
