Bitcoin Tests Critical Support — Institutions Keep Buying

Bitcoin is trading around $63,200, with Ethereum near $1,820. Analysts widely identify the $60,000–$63,000 range as a key psychological and technical support zone for the near term. A sustained hold above this level could squeeze short positions and attract fresh demand, while a break below $60k may open the path toward $50,000–$47,000. Importantly, analysts note the current decline is driven by leverage reduction rather than full-scale capitulation — a sign of a more orderly correction. Bitcoin's 200-week moving average sits near $58,500, a historically reliable support level.
